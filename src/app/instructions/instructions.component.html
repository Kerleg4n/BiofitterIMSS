Welcome to the basics of Biofitter!

In this page you will learn how to use Biofitter webpage in few steps:

Step 1: Choose a file (in tsv format) and upload it

Step 2: Every time you upload or delete data sets you need to redraw plot: hiding, updating and showing the chart with the respective buttons

Step 3: Your files are displayed automatically in the Data Sets section

Step 4: Select between different models to work with

Step 5: Click on fit button to manifest the parameters of your data sets
